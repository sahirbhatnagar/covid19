0.1 Log Ratios

Let \(y\) be the cumulative number of cases by day \(t\).

\[\begin{align*} \log(y) & = \beta_0 + \beta_1 t \\ \end{align*}\]

$_1 is the percent change in daily cases

\[\begin{align*} \log(y_{t+1}) - \log(y_{t}) & = \beta_0 + \beta_1 (t+1) - (\beta_0 + \beta_1 t) \\ & = \beta_1 \\ \log(\frac{y_{t+1}}{y_t}) & = \beta_1 \\ \log(\frac{y_t + y_{t+1} - y_t}{y_t}) & = \beta_1 \\ 1 + \log(\frac{y_{t+1} - y_t}{y_t}) & = \beta_1 \\ \end{align*}\]

pacman::p_load(locfit)
